How to Play Rope Rescue

Basic Controls

Use your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to draw ropes between anchor points. Click and drag to create a rope between two points.

On mobile devices, the game supports touch controls and works with both portrait and landscape orientations.

Game Objective

Your goal is to rescue the character(s) by creating a safe path using ropes. The rescued character must reach the designated safe zone.

Be careful of obstacles and hazards! Some levels require precise rope placement and timing to succeed.

Tips & Strategies

  • • Think about the physics before placing ropes
  • • Sometimes fewer ropes work better than many
  • • Watch how objects interact with each other
